This Eclipse plugin adds a new menuitem to your context menu to switch on and off visual/virtual word wrap.

Update site: http://ahtik.com/eclipse-update/

Warning! Word Wrap does not work properly with line numbers, line highlight etc! But this error is just temporary - after turning wrap off you should have everything back to normal!
